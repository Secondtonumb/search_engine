Personal Profile

Guotai Chi , who is a financial professor , doctoral supervisor, doctor of management science and engineering, works in Faculty of Management and Economics of Dalian University of Technology. Main research area: assets pricing; financial engineering and financial mathematics; investment risks management. Main research directions: bank assets and liabilities management; credit risks rating; loan pricing; the trading risks management of future goods and so on. As a leader , based on the above research directions, he has presided 9 National Natural Science Fund projects (71471027、71171031、70571010、70471055、70142008、70042002、79942009、79770011、71731003) among which one National Natural Science Fund grand project (71731003) is included and 2 National Social Science Fund projects(06&ZD039、04BJY082) among which one National Social Science Fund grand project (06&ZD039) is included. Beyond that, he has accomplished many consulting projects. For example, the small business credit risk rating and loan pricing systems for commercial banks like China Postal Savings Bank、 Dalian Bank and so on, the credit rating system for the farmers’ small fiduciary loans , the merchants’ small loan credit rating system , bank credit risk rating system and evaluation system for classifying loans into five categories. As the first author or corresponding author (graduate first, mentor second) ,he has published over 110 papers in class A important academic journals defined by the management science department in National Natural Science Foundation and published many essays in international journals ,many of those are SSCI, SCI, EI retrieval. Also, he has published 17 academic works and textbooks, including a national Twelfth Five-Year Plan textbook “investment risk management”, a Liaoning province Twelfth Five-Year Plan textbook “financial management”. Besides that, he has 2 national invention patent (Patent no. : ZL 2012 1 0201461.6 and ZL 2012 1 0201114.3) authorized by state in the field of credit rating the core technology and algorithm.
