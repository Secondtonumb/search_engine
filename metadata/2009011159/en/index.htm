Personal Profile

2004年于德国哥廷根大学（University of Goettingen）获博士学位。主要从事金属有机配合物的合成，表征及应用，例如金属配合物药物的合成，药用高分子材料的制备等。发表SCI收录的论文多篇，其中3篇发表在Angew. Chem. Int. Ed.上，2篇发表在J. Am. Chem. Soc.上，部分代表性论文见下文。

2 Di Li, Ying Peng(*), Chao Geng, Kaipeng Liu, Dexu Kong, Well-controlled ring-opening polymerization of cyclic esters initiated by dialkylaluminum β-diketiminates. Dalton Trans., 2013, 42: 11295-11303.
3 Yanhui Chen, Litao Liu, Ying Peng, Pingping Chen, Yi Luo, Jingping Qu(*), Unusual thiolate-bridged diiron clusters bearing the cis-HN=NH ligand and their reactivities with terminal Alkynes, J. Am. Chem. Soc., 2011, 133: 1147-1149.
4 Ying Peng, Hongjun Fan, Vojtech Jancik, Herbert W. Roesky(*), Herbst-Irmer, Regine. [LAl(µ-S3)2AlL]: A homobimetallic derivative of the sulfur crown S8. Angew. Chem., Int. Ed. Engl., 2004, 43(45): 6190-6192.
