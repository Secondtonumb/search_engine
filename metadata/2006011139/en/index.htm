Personal Profile

Lijun Jin, associate professor of Chemical Processing and Energy, received a Ph.D degree from Dalian University of Technology in 2006, and then joined Prof.Hu's group. The research interests are focused on the fundenmental study of coal structure and conversion, hydrogen production by catalytic decomposition of CH4 and CO2 reforming of CH4, Preparation and application of porous materials. As a PI, he undertook 3 projects of the Natural Scientific Foundation of China, 2 projects of Natural Scientific Foundation of Liaoning province, 3 projects of the Fundamental Research Funds for the Central Universities. He has published 70 peer-reviewed articles with about 900 citations.
