Personal Profile

Jian-Jun (Leo)Wang is a professor of the Faculty of Management and Economics of Dalian University of Technology in China and a postdoctoral researcher at the Business College of East Carolina University from 2012 to 2013. He completed his Ph.D. in Management Science and Engineering at Dalian University of Technology. His active research areas are healthcare delivery, IT outsourcing, operation management, service management, and data analytics. His publications have appeared in journals such as Information & Management, International Journal of Production Economics, International Journal of Logistics Management, Information Sciences, Journal of the Operational Research Society, Computers & Industrial Engineering, Computers & Operations Research, Journal of Manufacturing Systems, and International Journal of Advanced Manufacturing Technology. He has also presented several papers at AMCIS, ICMB, and other conferences. He served as a reviewer for a number of international refereed academic journals and refereed conferences.
